Tostones con Salami

Description:

English: Tostones are a delicious meal or snack for the whlie family! combine it with any sauce you'd like and some salami for a simple and quick snack!

Español: Los Tostones son una comida o una botana bien deliciosa para todo el familia! combínelos con cualquier salsa que gustarias para una botana fácil y rápida!

Ingredients:

  • 1 plantain
  • 1/2 piece of salami
  • Any cooking oil
  • Optional: Ketchup or some other sauce

Steps:

  1. Heat a pan up over a stove for a minute on medium heat
  2. Add your oil of choice
  3. Peel the plantain starting from the top of the stem
  4. Chop up the plantain into slices about 1/2 inch thick or less
  5. Add your plantain slices onto the pan and let it fry for until you see the bottom of the slices become a light yellow
  6. Flip your Tostones and let it cook until the other side becomes a light yellow
  7. Take the slices off of the pan and get a cup
  8. Smash the plantain Slices
  9. Refry both sides until both sides become a gliden yellow
  10. Fry your salami on the same oil and cook both sides until ready

    11. Enjoy! Disfruitar!
