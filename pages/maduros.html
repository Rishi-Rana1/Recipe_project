Maduros

Description:

English: Maduro prepared plantains are a sweet treat for the whole family!

Español: Plátanos preparado maduro son dulces para todo el familia!

Ingredients:

  • 2 very ripe (yellow!) plantains
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • Optional: salt

Steps:

  1. Heat a pan up over a stove for a minute on medium heat
  2. Add vegetable oil on pan
  3. Peel the plantain starting from the top of the stem
  4. Chop up the plantain into slices about 1/2 inch thick
  5. Cook each side until they are golden yellow/brownish

    6. Optional:

  6. Add salt

    7. Enjoy! Disfruitar!
