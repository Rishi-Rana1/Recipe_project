Mangu

Description:

English: Mangu is a commonly eaten breakfast or morning food eaten in the Dominican Republic! Its a fluffy and tasty mashed plantain dish!

Spanish: Mangu es un desayuno comunmente consumido en la Republica Dominicana! es un pur√© de platano macho esponjoso y sabroso!

Ingredients:

  • 2 plantains
  • 3/2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 tablespoons butter or olive oil
  • Optional: onions, salami, and viniger

Steps:

  1. Boil a pot of water at high heat
  2. Peel the plantain starting from the top of the stem
  3. Add the peeled plantains into the boiling water and make sure all the plantains are completely covered in boiling water
  4. Add salt into the water and boil until the plantains are tender
  5. Take out the plantains and mash them with a fork in a bowl while adding the butter/oil and room tempurature water
  6. Mash until you get a smooth puree with no lumps

    7. Optional:

  7. Fry the onions and salami
  8. add viniger and salt to the onions
  9. Combine them all together as seen in the picture
  10. Enjoy! Disfruitar!
